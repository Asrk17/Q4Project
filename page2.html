CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE
Juan Crisóstomo Ibarra y Magsalin
  • Crisóstomo Ibarra is the main character in the story.
  • He is the fiance of Maria Clara.
  • He is highly educated and idealistic.
Maria Clara
  • Maria Clara is the fiancee of Crisostomo Ibarra.
  • Raised as the daughter of Capitan Tiago.
  • She is sweet, modest, and kind.
Father Damaso
  • Damaso was the former curate of San Diego.
  • He is the real father of Maria Clara.
  • He is hungry for power and very arrogant.
Captain Tiago
  • Father of Maria Clara.
  • He is the richest person in Binondo.
  • He is said to be divine and religious.
Pilosopo Tasio
  • People regarded him as a crazy because of his unconventional beliefs.
  • He became a local adviser of the town.
  • He is smart and secretive.
