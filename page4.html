Cigarettes After Sex

The dreamy and melancholy music of the atmospheric pop band "Cigarettes After Sex" has brought them a lot of praise. Greg Gonzalez founded the band in 2008, and its dreamy melodies, personal lyrics, and hypnotic atmosphere are what define their music.

Their 2017 self-titled debut album won praise from listeners and critics alike for its slow-paced yet intriguing tunes that captivated audiences.

The band's music mainly focuses on themes of reflection, love, and longing, evoking an eerie yet nostalgic atmosphere. Their melodies are made even more delicate and intimate by Greg Gonzalez's intimate vocals.

"Cigarettes After Sex" gained a fan base thanks to songs like "K." and "Nothing's Gonna Hurt You Baby," which captivate listeners with their rich musical textures and emotional narratives.

Since making their debut, "Cigarettes After Sex" has not stopped captivating audiences with its unique sound. They create beautiful, evocative music that carries listeners away to a place of reflection and longing. Their music has a timeless appeal which attracts all listeners.
